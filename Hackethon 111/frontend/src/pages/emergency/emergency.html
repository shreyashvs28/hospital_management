What To Do In An Emergency

1. Stay Calm

Try to remain calm and assess the situation. Do not move the person if a neck or spine injury is suspected.

2. Call for Help

Call our emergency numbers immediately. Provide your location and a clear description of the emergency.

3. Provide First Aid

If you are trained, provide basic first aid. Do not give the person anything to eat or drink.
