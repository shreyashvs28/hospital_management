About Blood-Drop

Blood donation is a safe and noble act where a healthy person voluntarily donates about 350–450 ml of blood. This donated blood is separated into red blood cells, plasma, and platelets, which means a single donation can save up to three lives. Anyone between 18–65 years of age, weighing at least 45 kg, and in good health can donate.
